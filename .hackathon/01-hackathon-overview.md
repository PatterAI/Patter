# Stanford x DeepMind Hackathon -- Comprehensive Event Breakdown

## Event Details

- **Full Name:** Stanford x DeepMind Hackathon: Build. Ship. Win up to $5M in Seed Funding
- **Date:** April 12, 2026, 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM PT
- **Location:** Stanford campus (in-person) + remote/online option
- **Registration:** Free
- **Organizers:** GDG Stanford, Google DeepMind
- **Prerequisites Deadline:** Friday, April 10, 2026, 10:00 PM PST

---

## Competition Tracks

### Track 1 -- In-Person (Stanford Campus)

- 3-hour build sprint on site
- Prize pool: $60K in GCP credits + FastShot.ai Gemini Credits
- Access to live finals stage, networking, and VC meetings

### Track 2 -- Online (Remote)

- Work on your own schedule
- Submit by end of day April 12, 2026
- Prize pool: $10K in GCP credits
- Same judging consideration as in-person participants

## Judging Criteria

| # | Criterion | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | Technical functionality and feasibility | Does the product work? Is the tech approach sound and buildable? |
| 2 | Innovation and creative approach | How novel is the solution? Does it push boundaries? |
| 3 | Real-world applicability and user value | Does it solve a real problem for real users? |
| 4 | Market potential and venture viability | Is there a business here? Can it scale? |
| 5 | Go-to-market traction via social engagement | Demo videos must be public on YouTube. Likes, shares, and views count during the hackathon and for 2 weeks post-event. |

---

## Judging Process

1. **Semi-finals** -- Async panel review of all submissions
2. **Finals** -- Top 5 teams present live on stage (5-minute pitch + 5-minute Q&A)
